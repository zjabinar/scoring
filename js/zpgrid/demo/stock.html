Zapatec AJAX Grid - Stock Performance Example

A basic example showing a grid of the performance of the stock of various companies.
  • Sorted initially by first column in descending order.
  • You can click on the header of each column and sort in ascending order.
  • Clicking again sorts in descending order.
  • You can designate the data type of the columns as text, integer, date, or floating point and the grid will sort your data accordingly.
  • The grid is constructed from a standard HTML table.
